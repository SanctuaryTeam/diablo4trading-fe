SANCTUARY - TRADING

YOU ARE REQUIRED TO READ THIS BEFORE ENTERING THE DIABLO 4 SANCTUARY TRADING WEBSITE

  • Do not use Discord DMs or in-game chat to complete your trade. (This can hinder the process of a Scam Investigation.)
  • Do not trade anything that would be deemed RMT. (Services outside of the game, Real-life currency, Etc.)
  • Be sure to list everything for sale that you intend to sell. (Do not sell Mystery Bundles, Package Bundles, Etc.)
  • Do not falsely advertise any of your items. Please be accurate with the details of item stats.
  • Do not offer any items or gold that you do not have.
  • Do not scam any member within our Discord.
  • Do not offer Accounts, BattleNet Balances, Out-of-Network Items.
  • Do not abuse, manipulate or cheat any aspect of this Trading Ground.
  • Follow all Diablo 4 TOS/Code of Conduct when trading within our community.
